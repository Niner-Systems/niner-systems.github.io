Save Money With Only One Headset

Why spend extra money on a gaming headset when you can get more familiar with the incredible (and expensive) headset you already have. Building a computer specific for your simulator? Forego the cost of both the sound card and second headset with this adapter and spend the extra on a better graphics card. Mount the adapter within easy reach — unlike competitive products that are more expensive and leave wires hanging around.

Also works with iPads / iPhones / Android Devices

In addition to working with Mac, Windows, and Linux, we also work when connected to iPhones, iPads, and Android devices with USB Type C ports. Flying commercial, but taking your GA headset with you on vacation? You can use it for movies or games on the go. We know you are secretly hoping they also come over the intercom to ask: "Is there another pilot on board?" — so you'll be ready to go too.

Preflight Your Headset Configuration

Everything has gotten more high tech, including your headset. Your preflight should start at home. Verify your bluetooth connections to your iPad and phone are correct, test notifications from ForeFlight are working, make sure your hours flying straight and level won't have you troubleshooting why your phone is not connecting, and of course practice on VatSim/IVAO/PilotEdge with your own headset.

Powered by USB

General Aviation headsets require power for the microphone to work. 9er Systems' USB2GA uses power from the USB port to enable the microphone without the need for batteries.
