Connect your aviation headset to your simulator

Practice with the same headset you fly with

Powered by USB

General Aviation headsets require power for the microphone to work. 9er Systems' GA2USB uses power from the USB port to enable the microphone without the need for batteries.

Designed, manufactured, and supported in the USA

Designed and supported by engineers in Lexington KY and Knoxville TN, manufactured and assembled in Asheville NC. You spent a lot of money on your headset, you should feel confident that the devices you connect them to will keep them safe with high design and manufacturing standards.

Quality Assurance from the owners

The owners of 9er Systems use GA2USB with their own headsets. They have tested and actively use it with their personal Bose A20's, Lightspeed Zulu3's, David Clark ONE-X's, and some 30+ year old FlightCom's. Whether you have ANR or not, the 9er Systems GA2USB device is safe and compatible.
