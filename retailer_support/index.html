Product Description:

9er Systems USB2GA Headset Adapter allows pilots to connect their dual plug aviation headset directly to any computer or mobile device. The device registers itself as a headset on the computer and provides the required impedance matching and phantom power to GA microphones. The case design lends itself to being mounted, via 3M adhesive tape (provided) or screws (not included) to a desk or other simulator control allowing cables to be neatly organized. Whether connected to a computer or mobile device, the USB standard connection and drivers allow for Plug N Play use of GA headsets. All necessary power is provided by the USB host (computer) and no batteries are required. Use with X-Plane, MSFS, or any other program - including Zoom, just be sure to change your background to a picture of the interior of your plane. Headsets with Bluetooth capabilities can be simultaneously connected to an EFB while connected to the 9er Systems USB2GA Adapter.
