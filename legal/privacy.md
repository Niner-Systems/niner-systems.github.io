<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>

<p>9er Systems, LLC (also referred to as "9erSystems") ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the SecondSeater application (also referred to as "Second Seater," "2nd Seater," or "2ndSeater") and related services (the "Service"). 9er Systems is a registered trademark. <h2 class="left-aligned">1. Information We Collect</h2>

<p>We collect several types of information to provide and improve the Service:</p>

<h3>1.1 Account Information</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Name:</strong> Your full name as provided during registration</li>
    <li><strong>Email Address:</strong> Used for account authentication, communications, and account recovery</li>
    <li><strong>Phone Number:</strong> Used for account verification and emergency contact purposes</li>
    <li><strong>Password:</strong> Stored in encrypted form for account security</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.2 Aircraft Information</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Aircraft type, make, and model</li>
    <li>Aircraft registration number (N-number or equivalent)</li>
    <li>Aircraft equipment and avionics configuration</li>
    <li>Performance data and specifications</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.3 Audio Recordings</h3>

<div class="highlight-box">
<strong>Important Notice:</strong> The Service records audio in the aircraft cockpit. This includes ATC communications received over the radio as well as conversations between aircraft occupants transmitted through the intercom system. These intercom conversations may include private discussions that are not broadcast over public radio frequencies.
</div>

<ul>
    <li><strong>ATC Communications:</strong> Audio received from Air Traffic Control over aircraft radio frequencies</li>
    <li><strong>Pilot Transmissions:</strong> Your radio transmissions to ATC and other aircraft</li>
    <li><strong>Intercom Audio:</strong> Conversations between aircraft occupants transmitted through the aircraft intercom system, which may include private discussions not otherwise broadcast publicly</li>
    <li><strong>Cockpit Ambient Audio:</strong> Other sounds captured by the microphone during recording sessions</li>
    <li><strong>Transcriptions:</strong> Text transcriptions generated from audio recordings</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.4 Location and Flight Data</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>GPS Coordinates:</strong> Latitude, longitude, and altitude data collected during flights</li>
    <li><strong>Flight Track:</strong> Complete flight path from takeoff to landing</li>
    <li><strong>Speed and Heading:</strong> Ground speed, vertical speed, and aircraft heading</li>
    <li><strong>Timestamps:</strong> Date and time information associated with all location data</li>
    <li><strong>Airport Information:</strong> Departure and destination airports, frequencies used</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.5 Device and Usage Information</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Device type, operating system, and version</li>
    <li>Application version and configuration</li>
    <li>Feature usage patterns and frequency</li>
    <li>Error logs and diagnostic data</li>
    <li>IP address and general location derived from IP</li>
</ul>

<h3>1.6 Payment Information</h3>
<ul>
    <li>We use third-party payment processors (such as Stripe) to handle subscription payments</li>
    <li>We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers</li>
    <li>We receive and store transaction confirmations, billing addresses, and payment history</li>
</ul>
