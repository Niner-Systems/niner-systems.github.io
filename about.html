About 9er Systems

Aviation audio products — engineered, produced, and supported in the USA.

Engineers Who Fly

9er Systems exists because Pete saw a couple of products on the market and felt every one of them fell short of his expectations and desired feature set — so he convinced his lifelong friend of thirty years, John, to help him create something better. Together they cover the engineering and business disciplines that go into a product like this — from circuit design and firmware to the desktop software that ties it together, all the way through supply chain, inventory, marketing, and the e-commerce that gets it into customers' hands.

They initially tested their designs on Pete's own headset and simulator, but have since built up a variety of test components and equipment that let them validate each revision against the wider range of headsets and configurations their customers actually use. They sign off on every revision, and personally assemble, test, and package every adapter that leaves Knoxville, TN. If you have a question or a problem with one of their products, the email you send goes directly to both of them.
