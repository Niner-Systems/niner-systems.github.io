Built for the 6-Pin Lemo plug

If your headset uses the round 6-pin Lemo connector — the panel-powered plug — USB2Lemo is the version you want. The same mountable enclosure, the same USB Type C cable in the box, just the right jack on the front.

Save Money With Only One Headset

Why spend extra money on a gaming headset when you can get more familiar with the incredible (and expensive) headset you already have. Building a computer specific for your simulator? Forego the cost of both the sound card and second headset with this adapter and spend the extra on a better graphics card. Mount the adapter within easy reach — unlike competitive products that are more expensive and leave wires hanging around.

Also works with iPads / iPhones / Android Devices

In addition to working with Mac, Windows, and Linux, USB2Lemo works with iPhones, iPads, and Android devices over USB Type C. Flying commercial but taking your headset with you? Use it for movies or games on the go — with the same headset you actually fly with.

Powered by USB

USB2Lemo supplies the same panel power your headset expects, drawn from your computer's USB port — no batteries to swap, no wall adapter to misplace. Plug in the USB-C cable, plug in your headset, fly.
